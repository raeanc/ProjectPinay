PROJECT PINAY

Project Pinay is a self-initiated overseas Values-In-Action (VIA) project under Rotaract Club of Singapore that focuses on educating youths in the Philippines on sexual education and puberty. Founded in 2021, the project has since been continued by successive teams and is currently in its fifth edition.
