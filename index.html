Project Pinay

According to the United Nations Population Fund, 1 in 10 Filipino girls aged 15-19 are already mothers or expecting. We want to change things.

WHO ARE WE?

Project Pinay is a self-initiated overseas project under Rotaract Club of Singapore that focuses on educating youths in the Philippines on puberty & sexual education, in hopes of helping them make more informed choices.

OUR MISSION

To educate & empower Filipino youths by providing accessible sexual education, puberty awareness, & menstrual hygiene knowledge through interactive lessons & community engagement.

WHY "PINAY"?

The term "Pinay" is a colloquial term used to refer to a Filipino woman or girl, hence Project Pinay celebrates Filipino girls & women, with our name. It also reflects our focus on empowering them through education, awareness & advocacy.

WHY THE PHILIPPINES?

Teenage pregnancy in the Philippines remains alarmingly high, with UNFPA echoing NEDA & POPCOM in describing it as a "national emergency.", citing how only 28% of Filipino adolescents have access to comprehensive sexuality education in schools.

PAST RUNS

Founded in 2021, the project has since been continued by successive teams & is currently in its fifth edition. Through physical lessons & interactive games, we hope to introduce the concept of sexual education & puberty in a light-hearted manner such that youths are able to better understand & be receptive to such sensitive topics.
