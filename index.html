PROJECT PINAY

Empowering Filipino youths through sexual education.

WHO ARE WE?

Project Pinay is a self-initiated overseas Values-In-Action (VIA) project under Rotaract Club of Singapore that focuses on educating youths in the Philippines on sexual education and puberty.

OUR MISSION

To educate and empower Filipino youths by providing accessible sexual education, puberty awareness, and menstrual hygiene knowledge through interactive lessons and community engagement.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2021, the project has since been continued by successive teams and is currently in its fifth edition. Through physical lessons and interactive games, we hope to introduce the concept of sexual education and puberty in a light-hearted manner such that youths are able to better understand and be receptive to such sensitive

WHY "PINAY"?

The term “Pinay”, which is a colloquial term used to refer to a Filipino woman or girl. celebrates Filipino girls and women, reflecting our focus on empowering them through education, awareness, and advocacy.”
