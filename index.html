Project Pinay

1 in 10 Filipino girls aged 15-19 are already mothers or expecting. We want to change things.

WHO ARE WE?

Project Pinay is a self-initiated overseas project under Rotaract Club of Singapore that focuses on educating youths in the Philippines on puberty and sexual education in hopes of helping them make more informed choices.

OUR MISSION

To educate and empower Filipino youths by providing accessible sexual education, puberty awareness, and menstrual hygiene knowledge through interactive lessons and community engagement.

WHY "PINAY"?

The term "Pinay", which is a colloquial term used to refer to a Filipino woman or girl, celebrates Filipino girls and women, reflecting our focus on empowering them through education, awareness, and advocacy.

WHY THE PHILIPPINES?

Teenage pregnancy in the Philippines remains alarmingly high, with UNFPA echoing NEDA and POPCOM in describing it as a "national emergency."
This is partly due to Limited sexual education, with only 28% of Filipino adolescents having access to comprehensive sexuality education in schools according to the UNFPA.
We help to solve part of the problem by empowering Filipino youths with sexual education, increasing awareness.
